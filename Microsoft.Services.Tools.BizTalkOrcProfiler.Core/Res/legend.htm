BizTalk 2016 Orchestration Coverage Report
 

Report Guide

This reports gives a consolidated view of orchestration tracking data for a specified period of time to help developers get an idea of how their orchestrations are being processed and how much coverage they are getting in their testing. Orchestration shapes are color coded according to their coverage levels.
  • Red shapes have never been hit.
  • Green Shapes have been hit and exited. Every time they have been hit they have completed successfully.
  • Blue shapes have been hit but have only been exited a set number of times. E.g. the shape may have been entered, called a custom component and thrown an exception on pass 1, but completed successfully on pass2 - making the overall success rate 50%.
